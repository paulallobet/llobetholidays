Calella

The apartment is located in the center of Calella, a coastal municipality in the Maresme region. The city is located 50 km from Barcelona and Girona, its geographical location, as well as the communication routes, become a key factor for all kinds of excursions and activities. This fact makes it an ideal tourist destination.

Canet

The apartment is located in Canet de Mar, a municipality open to the sea and surrounded by mountains, a beautiful contrast of shades in blues and greens, where you can enjoy clean and quiet beaches and an ideal natural environment to make routes through the Mountain on foot or by bike. In addition, between 3 and 30 km we can find 30 golf courses, including two of the PGA.

Apart from highlighting the excellent climate, the extensive beach area, over two and a half kilometers, and the forests surrounding the municipality, Canet de Mar is the town where, in less space there is the greatest modernist heritage. Canet is also known for its varied and quality gastronomic offer, with more than 30 restaurants specializing in Mediterranean and homemade cuisine. The products of the garden, land and sea are very present.
